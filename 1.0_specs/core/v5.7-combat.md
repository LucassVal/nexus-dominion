## Combat Formula
```
playerATK = (baseATK + weaponATK + accessoryATK + mountBonus) × (1 + combatSkill × 0.04)
playerDEF = (baseDEF + armorDEF + accessoryDEF + mountDEF) × (1 + combatSkill × 0.03)
damage = max(1, floor(playerATK - enemyDEF + random(-2,3)))
crit: 8% + combatSkill × 2% → 1.8x damage
```

## Mount Combat Bonuses
- War Horse: +3 ATK, +3 DEF
- Flee bonus: mount.spd × 0.15 (cap 0.45)
- Griffin gives +45% flee chance

## Equipment Durability
- Weapons lose 1 durability per attack
- Armor loses 1 durability per hit taken
- At 0: equipment breaks, removed from slot
- No repair system (buy new)

## Random Encounters
- 6% chance per tick while moving
- Bounty hunters spawn if bounty > $500 (4% chance)
- Army soldier encounter after joining army (timed)
