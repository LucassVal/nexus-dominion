## Mechanics

### Movement Speed
```
speed = 3 × terrainMod × mountTerrainMod
```
- Base: 3 px/tick
- Terrain modifier: water=0, plains=1.0, forest=0.7, mountain=0.4, desert=0.8
- Mount modifier: mount.spd OR mount.terrain[tile] (per-terrain override)

### Durability
- Each mount has `dur` (max durability)
- Decays 1 point every 9 ticks while moving
- At 0: mount breaks, player loses bonuses
- Repair: 15% of original cost at any city
- HUD shows: `SPD 2.0x [145/200]`

### Pathfinding
- Flying mounts (griffin, airship): `findPath(sx,sy,tx,ty,fly=true)` ignores terrain
- Ground mounts: standard pathfinding (blocked by water/mountain)

### Combat
- War Horse: +3 ATK, +3 DEF
- Flee bonus: `fleeChance = 0.25 + stealth*0.06 + mount.spd*0.15` (cap 0.45)
- Example: Griffin gives +45% flee chance

### Cargo Bonus
- Wagon: +50, Carriage: +30, Airship: +100
- Applied to caravan if player has one
- Dismount checks capacity (blocks if overflow)
- buyMount auto-dismounts previous mount
