## Leveling
- XP from combat (enemy.xp) and items (tome, scroll)
- Threshold: lvl × 100 XP per level
- Level up: +1 skill point, +8 MHP, restore 8 HP
- Max practical level: ~20 (2000 XP total)

## Inventory
- Base capacity: 30 items
- Caravan: +50 capacity ($500)
- Total weight = sum of item weights
- Food spoilage: items with `per` field decay
- Equipment: weapon, armor, accessory slots with durability
