## Country Investments
- `investInCountry(country, amount)`: Invest gold, 30-day term, 5-20% ROI
- Returns principal + interest after 30 days
- Passive income from embassies (10-50 gold every 3 days)

## Country AI (countryAI)
- Runs every 8 days
- War: declared if relations < -20 (25% chance)
- Peace: auto-peace if treasury depleted or 8% random
- Army movement: toward enemy cities during war, patrol otherwise
- City capture: 25% chance when army reaches enemy city
- Population growth: 0.3% × stability / day
- Relations: decay/grow over time, affected by embassies

## Military
- Each country has 1-3 armies (500-3000 troops)
- Armies fight each other (power = size × random factor 0.8-1.2)
- Can capture enemy cities
- Player can join army for $200 bonus + combat encounters
