## PART C: TRANSPORT NETWORK

### Trains
- Connect cities within same country (pop > 150K)
- Cost: $50-150 depending on distance
- Time: instant (teleport after cost)
- Available every day

### Ships
- Connect port cities (cities on water-adjacent tiles)
- Cross-country routes
- Cost: $75-250
- Time: instant
- Can carry cargo (caravan goods transfer)

### Caravan (Player)
- Buy for $500 at any city
- Upgrade: +25 capacity per $300
- Hire guards: $20/day each
- Speed: 70% of player speed
- Can be attacked by bandits

### Public Transport (NPC)
- AI traders and NPCs use same network
- Creates dynamic population movement

## PART E: STOCK MARKET v4

### Public Companies
- 8-12 listed companies
- Dynamic prices based on: country GDP, wars, events, player actions
- Can buy/sell shares
- Hostile takeover at >50%

### Private Companies (Player)
- Found a private company: $20000
- Choose sector (finance, tech, military, logistics, general)
- Operates in cities like v2
- Revenue daily
- Can take PUBLIC (IPO) for $50000 → get lump sum + ongoing dividends
- Can keep PRIVATE → full control, all revenue

## PART F: DECAY SYSTEM

Runs every game tick (3 seconds):

1. **Weapon durability**: -1 per combat round. At 0: weapon breaks, -50% ATK. Repair at blacksmith ($cost)
2. **Armor durability**: -1 per hit taken. At 0: breaks. Repair at armorer.
3. **Food spoilage**: Each food item has spoilDay. When game day > spoilDay: item removed, notification.
4. **Road condition**: Roads lose 0.5% condition/day. Below 50%: speed penalty. Countries repair automatically if treasury > 50000.
5. **Building maintenance**: City buildings lose 0.2%/day. Below 30%: stability penalty.
6. **Market staleness**: Unsold goods lose 1% value/day. Encourages trade flow.
